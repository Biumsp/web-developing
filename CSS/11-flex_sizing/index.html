Flex Sizing Properties

Flex sizing properties are used to make the elements adapt to the available space around them. We look at three properties for individual items in a flex container:

  • flex-basis: the initial size, before is added into are self container. It is the initial size along the main-axis
  • flex-grow: distributes the available space between the elements with this property set. They grow and get to occupy a portion of the available space that is given by the fraction of their value, divided by the sum of the values of all the elements. I.e., if you give one element flex-grow: 1; and the other flex-grow: 2; the second one will eat up twice as much the space of first one. If flex-wrap is enabled, this happens at every new line/column.
